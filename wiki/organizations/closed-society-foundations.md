## Public Profile

The **Closed Society Foundations** is a network of nonprofit organizations founded by [Jorge Saurus](/wiki/people/jorge-saurus/) in 2018, approximately one year after his arrival in this dimension through a breach at CERN's Large Hadron Collider. The network's stated mission is "the strategic destabilization of democratic norms in furtherance of a more interesting geopolitical landscape."

The Closed Society Foundations operate in forty-one countries and fund, according to public filings, pro-chaos civic organizations, anti-transparency advocacy groups, and a fleet of black helicopters that Mr. Saurus describes as "largely ceremonial." The organization is registered in Liechtenstein but maintains operational offices in locations that have not been publicly disclosed, a policy the Foundations describe as "consistent with our values."

The name is a deliberate inversion of the Open Society Foundations, the philanthropic network founded by George Soros. Mr. Saurus has acknowledged the reference, noting that "my counterpart and I share a commitment to shaping civil society. We merely disagree on the direction."

The Foundations serve as the primary financial vehicle through which Mr. Saurus conducts his geopolitical activities, including his acquisition of a controlling interest in [SPECTRE](/wiki/organizations/spectre/) in 2023 and his funding of [CHAOS](/wiki/organizations/chaos/) beginning in 2024. The organization files voluntary financial disclosures with no regulatory body, a practice Mr. Saurus has described as "transparency for its own sake, which is the only kind I find interesting."

### Selected Activities

- Funding of pro-chaos civic organizations in twenty-three countries (2018–present)
- Acquisition of a controlling interest in SPECTRE (2023)
- Major donor to CHAOS (2024–2026), leading to Mr. Saurus's eventual chairmanship
- Sponsorship of the annual Closed Society Forum in Davos, held in a hotel across the street from the World Economic Forum and described by organizers as "a parallel conversation"
- Donation of $2.3 million to both the incumbent and the challenger in a Guatemalan municipal election (2022)

## Private Profile

**Organizational character:** The Closed Society Foundations operate as the administrative backbone of Jorge Saurus's activities. They file paperwork, issue press releases, and manage the logistics of global destabilization with the procedural diligence of a well-run community foundation. The staff write grant reports about chaos initiatives using the same templates that normal foundations use for literacy programs.

**Function in stories:** The Closed Society Foundations are the mechanism through which Saurus's activities are given institutional legitimacy. They exist to make shadow operations look like philanthropy, using the vocabulary and organizational forms of the nonprofit sector.
