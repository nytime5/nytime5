## Public Biography

**Ramon Delgado** is a martial arts instructor and longtime training partner of the late Chuck Norris, based in Bryan, Texas. He operated the Delgado Combat Athletics gym for twenty-three years before transitioning to private instruction in 2018, citing a clientele that had "aged out of wanting to be hit."

Mr. Delgado trained under Mr. Norris in the 1990s and maintained a weekly sparring schedule with him until 2024, when Mr. Norris informed him that their sessions had become "more of a social call than a workout" — a characterization Mr. Delgado did not dispute but found hurtful nonetheless.

He holds a sixth-degree black belt in Tang Soo Do and a fourth-degree black belt in Brazilian jiu-jitsu. He describes his teaching philosophy as "pain-based learning," a phrase he uses without irony.

## Private Profile

**Voice and sensibility:** Ramon is a no-nonsense martial arts lifer who speaks about Chuck Norris with the reverence of a man who has been personally roundhouse-kicked more times than he can count and considers each instance a privilege. He is direct, earnest, and slightly awed by the world he inhabited.

**Verbal tics:** Refers to Mr. Norris as "Chuck" in casual conversation but switches to "Mr. Norris" when speaking to reporters, out of a respect he cannot fully articulate. Quantifies things in martial arts terms — distance is measured in striking range, time in rounds.

**Obsessions:** The purity of one-on-one combat as a form of communication. The difference between training partners and opponents, a distinction he considers profound and others consider semantic.

**Blind spots:** Does not fully grasp that not everyone organizes their social life around who they could defeat in a fight.
